# **App Name**: Gym Progress

## Core Features:

- Initial Plan Generation: Generates a personalized basic training plan based on user input regarding experience, goals (strength/hypertrophy), weekly training days, and equipment limitations, gathered through a guided questionnaire.
- Progressive Overload Suggestions: Offers suggestions after each workout to increase weight or reps based on user performance and completion of sets/reps/weight. An AI tool will consider how easy or difficult the exercise appeared.
- Failure Set Logging and Adjustment: Allows users to log sets where they failed to complete the desired reps, and suggests lowering weight or reps for the next workout based on this record. The AI tool helps maintain safety and effectiveness.
- Data Visualization: Provides charts visualizing strength trends for various exercises or overall strength, along with cumulative training volume charts to help users understand their training workload.
- Personal Record (PR) Tracking: Automatically tracks and highlights the user's personal bests for each exercise (e.g., 1RM, max reps), providing a sense of accomplishment.
- Achievement Badges/Milestones: Automatically awards virtual badges or displays achievement notifications upon reaching specific training goals.
- Progress Reports: Automatically generates brief weekly/monthly training progress reports, summarizing training outcomes, highlighting personal bests, and providing encouragement or suggestions.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A vibrant and saturated blue (#29ABE2), promoting a sense of energy and motivation, crucial for fitness apps.
- Background color: A very light blue (#E5F5F9), providing a clean and unobtrusive backdrop that keeps the focus on the training data.
- Accent color: A complementary teal (#2D8B87), used for call-to-action elements and highlights to guide user interaction.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' sans-serif font to provide a modern, neutral look; appropriate for both headlines and body text.
- Use clean, modern icons to represent different exercises, progress metrics, and achievement badges.
- Emphasize data visualization with clear and concise charts, utilizing a dashboard-style layout for easy progress monitoring.
- Implement subtle animations and transitions to provide engaging feedback upon completion of exercises or achievement of milestones.
